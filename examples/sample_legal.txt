MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

1. Definitions
1.1 "Affiliate" means any entity controlling, controlled by, or under common control.
1.2 "Confidential Information" includes technical, financial, and customer records.

2. Data Protection
2.1 Provider shall implement encryption at rest and in transit.
2.2 Provider shall notify Customer within 72 hours of any data breach.

Annexure A: Security Controls
A) Access control logs retained for 365 days.
B) Quarterly penetration testing reports are mandatory.
